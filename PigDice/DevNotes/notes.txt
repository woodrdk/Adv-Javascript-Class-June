Objective:
Create a functional pig dice game
2 human players can play against each other

Rules:
- Players take turns rolling a single dice
- Player accumlates a turn total with each dice roll
- If player rolls a 1, they get 0 points for their turn
    and the next player starts their turn
- If player rolls 2 - 6, they die roll gets added to their
    turn total until they decide to hold and pass turn
    to next player
- Win Condition: First player to 100 points wins
